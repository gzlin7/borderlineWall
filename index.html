Tunnel66: isolation, together.

Tunnels connect many of MIT's buildings, and they can even connect a community in quarantine. Inspired by Corona Maison, Tunnel66 is a place to gather and showcase the unique cultures of current and prospective MIT students, in lieu of CPW. Click on a room to learn more about the artist!

Submissions are open; download the template.

Tunnel66 is created and maintained by Borderline, the student organization responsible for augmented reality murals in the tunnels under Building 66. Contact: borderline-team@mit.edu
